About Me

I work at Walt Disney World as a Lifeguard. I've also been a Recreation Coordinator, Lake Patrol, and Trainer at Caribbean Beach, Pop Century, and Art of Animation resorts and a Lifeguard Instructor contracted with Jeff Ellis & Associates.

I earned a B.S. in Computer Engineering from the University of Florida in 1986. The positions I've held include Senior Software Engineer, Director of Software Development, Architect and Technical Program Manager, and Development Director. The companies I worked for are Electronic Tele-Communications, Veritas Software, and Electronic Arts.

I'm a Girl Scout Troop Leader, Camporee Director, and Outdoor Skills Trainer.

I've been a Cub Scout Den Leader, Boy Scout Assistant Scoutmaster, and Venture Crew Advisor. I was an Assistant Scoutmaster at the National Scout Jamboree in 2005 and 2010. I was the Lead Crew Advisor for high adventure backpacking treks at Philmont Scout Ranch in 2007, 2009, and 2016. I was the Lead Crew Advisor for a canoe trek and a dog sled trek at Northern Tier National High Adventure Base in 2004 and 2005. I'm certified in wilderness first aid and advanced open water scuba diving.

I play the flute, baritone, and piano. I was in the University of Florida Gator Marching Band from 1981-1985.

I'm married with three children. My children are 13, 21, and 26 years old. I have one dog and 2 cats. My dog is a basset hound and beagle mix, also known as a "bagle hound". I was born in Plattsburgh, NY. I live in Lake Mary, FL.
